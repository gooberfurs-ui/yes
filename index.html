About me

I wanted this to feel gentle, honest, and easy to be in

I didn’t want a stiff portfolio. I wanted something that sounds like me and feels kind.

The heart of it

I’m Luna, and a lot of people know me from TikTok. I also put a lot of love into creating avatars for VRChat. I’m drawn to things that feel expressive, playful, dreamy, and full of personality, so I wanted my site to feel that way too.

  • I create under @GoodDoggoLuna
  • I make content and I make avatars
  • I keep coming back to the con scene every year

I care about being real

I’m open about living with bipolar disorder, ADHD, anxiety, and other mental health struggles because I don’t want to pretend I’m untouched by any of it. I’d rather be honest, soft, and sincere than sound perfect. The people who find comfort in that honesty matter a lot to me.

I want this to feel like a place where people can breathe a little, smile a little, and feel close to me.
